# Privacy

Parlour collects nothing.

- **No accounts, no sign-in.** There is no server to sign in to.
- **No network calls.** After the page loads, Parlour never talks to the
  internet. Every game runs entirely on your device — that's why it works
  offline.
- **No ads, no analytics, no trackers.** None. Ever.
- **Local only.** Any scores or preferences are stored in your browser on this
  device and never leave it. Clearing your browser data clears them.

It's a games cupboard, not a data business.
